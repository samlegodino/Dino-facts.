    Interesting facts about Plesiosaurus

    • Plesiosaurus was a plesiosaur, a type of marine reptile. It was not a dinosaur, though it coexisted with many dinosaurs.
    • The first Plesiosaurus fossil was discovered in 1821.
    • Existed from 228 million years ago to 61.6 million years ago
    • 938 different specimens have been found by paleontologists
