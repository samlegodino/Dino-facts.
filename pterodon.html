    Interesting facts about Pteroadoon

    • Pteranodon was a pterosaur, a type of flying reptile. It was not a dinosaur, though it lived during the same period.
    • Pteranodon lived during the Late Cretaceous and resided in North America.
    • Existed from 89.8 million years ago to 70.6 million years ago
    • 39 different specimens have been found by paleontologists
    • Was a piscivore
    • Had a body mass of approximately 20 kilograms (44 lb) to as high as 93 kilograms (205 lb).
