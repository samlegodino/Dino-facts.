    Interesting facts about Pliosaurus

    • Pliosaurus was a plesiosaur, a type of marine reptile. It was not a dinosaur, though it coexisted with many dinosaurs.
    • The first Pliosaurus fossil was discovered in 1841.
    • Existed from 189.6 million years ago to Cretaceous Period
    • 9 different specimens have been found by paleontologists
